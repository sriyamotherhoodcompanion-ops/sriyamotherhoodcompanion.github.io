Privacy Policy

SRIYA Motherhood Companion App  •  Last updated: June 21, 2026

SRIYA ("the App", "we", "us") is a pregnancy companion app designed to help mothers track their pregnancy journey with care and privacy in mind. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using SRIYA, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Important: SRIYA is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical advice.

1. Information We Collect

SRIYA collects information you choose to enter into the App in order to provide its core features. This information is stored securely in our database (Google Firebase) and is associated only with your individual account.

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address (used to create and sign in to your account)

1.2 Pregnancy & Health Information

The following information is entered voluntarily by you to power the App's tracking features:

  • Pregnancy profile details (due date, current week, mother's name)
  • Mood tracker entries
  • Symptom records
  • Blood pressure and blood sugar readings
  • Sleep, water intake, and weight tracking entries
  • Fetal movement / kick counter sessions
  • Contraction timer sessions
  • Journal entries (which may include photos you choose to attach)
  • Medical records you choose to upload (which may include photos of documents)
  • Meal plans and hospital bag checklists
  • Reminders you create

1.3 Emergency Contact Information

If you choose to use the Emergency Contacts feature, you may enter the name and phone number of another person (such as a partner, family member, or doctor). Please only enter this information with that person's knowledge.

1.4 Photos and Camera Access

SRIYA requests access to your device's camera and photo library only when you choose to attach a photo to a journal entry, event, medical record, or your profile picture. Photos are uploaded only when you actively select or take one — the App does not access your camera or photos at any other time.

1.5 Questions Sent to Our AI Advisor

If you use the "Ask a Question" feature, your question — along with your name, current pregnancy week, and trimester — is sent to a third-party AI service provider (Groq) to generate a response. See Section 3 for details.

1.6 App Reviews

If you submit a review or rating within the App, this is stored and associated with your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the App's tracking, journaling, and reminder features
  • To generate AI-powered answers to your pregnancy-related questions
  • To allow you to export your own journal entries and pregnancy records as PDF documents for your personal use
  • To process purchases of Paid or Premium plan access (see Section 4)
  • To respond to your feedback, reviews, or support requests

We do not use your information for advertising, and SRIYA does not display ads or sell your data to advertisers.

3. Sharing of Information with Third Parties

Third PartyWhat is sharedPurpose
Google Firebase All account and health-tracking data described in Section 1 Secure cloud storage and authentication for the App
Groq (AI service provider) Your typed question, mother's name, pregnancy week, and trimester To generate a response to your question via the "Ask a Question" feature
Gumroad None from within the App — purchase links open in your device's browser, where Gumroad's own privacy policy applies To process Paid/Premium plan purchases

We do not sell your personal or health information to any third party for any purpose, including advertising.

4. Purchases and Plan Unlocks

SRIYA offers optional Paid and Premium plans. Purchases are completed externally through Gumroad. After purchase, you receive an unlock code by email, which you enter in the App to unlock additional features. We do not receive or store your payment card details — these are handled entirely by Gumroad.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore), which applies encryption in transit and at rest. Access to your personal data within the App is restricted to your own account through Firebase security rules — other users cannot read or modify your data.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account remains active, or as needed to provide you the App's features. If you would like your account and associated data deleted, please contact us using the details in Section 9, and we will delete your data within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

SRIYA is intended for use by adults and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction).

8. Your Choices

  • You may edit or delete journal entries, tracker data, and other information you've entered, directly within the App, at any time
  • You may request a copy or deletion of your data by contacting us
  • You may decline camera/photo permission requests; doing so will only limit photo-attachment features and will not affect the rest of the App
